## Professional Summary 

Power Systems Engineer with 10+ years of experience spanning grid operation and maintenance, utility-scale substation construction, transmission & distribution network modeling, microgrid energy management systems, and grid-connected BESS integration. Proficient in end-to-end project execution, including electrical design review, equipment specification, construction support, commissioning, and energization for LV/MV utility and distributed energy projects. Strong expertise in large-scale power flow analysis using OpenDSS-Python, real-time control validation using Typhoon HIL, and advanced power system studies involving OPF, state estimation, and high-performance computational benchmarking. Experienced in MV/HV transformers, switchgear, grounding systems, protection and control, inverter-based resources, BESS plant controls, SCADA integration, and utility communication protocols including Modbus TCP/IP and IEC 61850. Demonstrated ability to collaborate effectively with utilities, EPC contractors, cross-functional engineering teams, and stakeholders to deliver reliable, standards-compliant, and resilient energy infrastructure solutions. Passionate about deploying intelligent and sustainable energy technologies to accelerate grid modernization and renewable energy integration.
